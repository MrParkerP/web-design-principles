The Greatest Game Ever by Arjun Terese
Katana Zero is perhaps one of the greatest indie games to ever have been made. The simplistic nature of the design allows the player the ability to get lost in the beautiful artwork and deceptively deep story. The fast paced cycle of gameplay also allows for more complex and diffivult levels designs without sacrificing replayability. One can imagine a larger scale game created with many of these choiced in mind. I long await the sequel to such a beautiful game...
The Worst Game Ever by Larissa Blaze
Katana Zero is perhaps one of the worst indie games ever have been made. For starters the controls are much too simple for my compplex mind, it felt like I was being treated like a child. Also, the fast pace and constant crippling dificulty made it no fun to play at all but rather felt like a death farm. One of the worst experiences with playing videogames I have ever had and I would not recommend it to anyone...

However, there is one thing that can unite all gamers, and that is games of course. Aided by a digital age, we are not hurt, but helped by isolation where we can truly unite...

Most recently we got the Switch, knocking expectations out of the water with a brand new Smash Bros game to enjoy. Once you login online though, the real pain starts. The Nintendo servers are the weak link of such a major franchise and must be amended...
